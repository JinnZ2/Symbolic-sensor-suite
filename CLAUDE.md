## Naming Conventions

- **Sensor JSON files:** `[acronym]_[domain].json` (e.g., `asos_ai_output.json`, `cld_collective.json`)
- **Sensor codes:** `SAAS-001`, `MDS-001`, `SMCS-001`, `ADS-001`
- **Python modules:** snake_case (`gaslight_index.py`, `consistency_guard.py`)
- **Python classes:** PascalCase (`GaslightDetector`, `ConsistencyGuard`)
- **Data/config files:** kebab-case or snake_case, **no spaces in filenames**
- **Glyphs:** Unicode symbol + semantic label (`🜔 Noise-as-Signal`, `〰 Whiplash`)
